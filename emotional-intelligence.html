Emotional Intelligence and IQ

What is emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is being able to manage your own emotions as well as emotions of others. Its also about being able to harness those emotions and utilizing it for task such as thinking and problem solving

    The 5 key components of emotional intelligence
  1. self awareness - self awareness is the ability to recognize you feelings, behaviors and thoughts. Self awarness doesnt develop at once. It beings in early childhood and as you mature you start to recognize and acknowledge their emotions and strengths and it keeps developing thorughout live.
  2. self regulation - self regulation is being able to understand your behaviors and feelings and being able to manage them.
  3. motivation - motivation is your about your needs and desires within each individual. Its about driving towards/acomploish a goal.
  4. empathy - being able to understand and share feelings openly with another and also being able to sense others peoples emotions.
  5. social skills - social skills are used to communicate with others regularly using many different ways like verbal, nonverbal, visual. Social skills are important because they help you communicate more effectiviely and effeciently.

How is it differet from IQ?

Ones iq is a measurement based of someones mental cognitive abbilites and is expressed as a number.

Why is it important?

It is important because you have the ability to control your feelings, emotions and deal with new circumstances. A high eq can also help you build new relations ships and reduce stress.
