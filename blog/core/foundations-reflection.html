Foundations Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and ego as a result of the core learning?

I've explored the area of identity and values. Before going into dev academy I wasn't to sure about my values and identity's as it has never crossed my mind. From the research I have gathered i think that I am on the right path to creating good values going into the future. good core values.

Using mindfulness meditation I have learnt that i am more calm and relaxed.

I learnt about the brain all the various ways that it can grow and learn.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self awareness in learning and programming.

Empathy is important in learning and programming because empathy lies at the heart of emotional intelligence and is about creating stronger bonds and being aware of our personal emotions. In programming its about viewing other peoples perspectives on things.

Self awareness in programming is important because when we are self aware we are able to recognize and strengths and weaknesses making us also be able to recognize where we have room to grow.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Neuroplasticity & Growth mindset. It showed me how little I know about the brain and its ability to grow. I've also learnt that I am able to use these core learning techniques to help better my learning.

What were the most challenging aspects of core learning?

Something that was hard was grasping the concepts but mainly because I was uninterested in the core learning. But I had eventually learnt how important it was

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

I think you guys are focusing on it because the tech industry isn't just looking for technical people they are look for people with core skills and the ability to interact and communicate with with others. I think that you guys arent only preparing us for the technical side of job but the core side.

Does the time you spent studying core learning feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead?

I think it was good it has taught me alot about myself I didnt know. I did find it a little boring at time but that is because I do prefer practicing programming. I think its necessary to learn but somehow make it a little more fun and engaging.
