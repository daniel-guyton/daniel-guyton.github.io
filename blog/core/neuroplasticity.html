Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Discuss what a growth mindset is and why it is relevant?

A growth mindset is where we believe that our brainpower can be improved with effort and the right plan/strategy. Its important because a growth mindset encourages risk taking without worrying about failure because mistakes are a chance to learn.

Continuing to practice and learn can create more solid connections in the brain.
